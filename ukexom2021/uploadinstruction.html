Instructions for contributed speakers

  1. Upload your recorded talks not exceeding 15 minutes to exoplanet-talks.org

  2. When uploading the talk, please use "UKEXOM2021" in keywords. This will allow us to easily find and differentiate the talk slot.

  3. After uploading the talk, share the talk link on this form -- Talk form.

  4. Deadline to upload the talk and share the link is 5th April 2021.

  5. If you have an image/plot to advertise your talk in Gather.Town, please email the plot/image to ukexom2021@contacts.bham.ac.uk
